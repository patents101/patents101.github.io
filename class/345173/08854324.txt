An information_processing_device that includes a display  a touch_panel that detects a gesture_operation  a memory that stores a correlation between each of a plurality of effects that can be applied to an object_displayed on the display and one of a plurality of gesture_operations  and a processor that applies one of the plurality of effects that corresponds to the detected_gesture_operation to an object_displayed on the display  and controls the display to display the object to which the one of the plurality_effects is applied.
