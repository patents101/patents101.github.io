In an embodiment  a processor includes a graphics_domain_including a graphics_engines each having at least one execution_unit. The graphics_domain is to schedule a touch_application_offloaded from a core_domain to at least one of the plurality of graphics_engines. The touch_application is to execute_responsive to an update to a doorbell_location in a system_memory_coupled to the processor  where the doorbell_location is written_responsive to a user_input to the touch_input_device.
