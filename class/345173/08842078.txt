A touch_display_device includes a display_panel_including a first surface and a second surface. The first surface is a displaying_surface of the display_panel  and the second surface is the other surface of the display_panel_opposite to the displaying_surface. The touch_display_device further includes a circuit_board_disposed on a side of the second surface of the display_panel  and a touch_panel_disposed between the display_panel and the circuit_board.
