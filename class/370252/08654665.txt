A mobile_terminal and a handover_method for the mobile_terminal are disclosed. A mobile_terminal according to an embodiment of the invention may include  a communication_unit_configured to receive from a base_station_multiple_frames  each belonging to one of two or more frame in a set_order of priority  and a control_unit_configured to control the mobile_terminal to perform a handover to a base_station_based on the occurrence_pattern of frame_types of the multiple_frames.
