A method for detecting a transmission_collision between a first wireless_communication_terminal and a second wireless_communication_terminal includes the following steps  when one retry_packet_transmitted from the first wireless_communication_terminal is received by the second wireless_communication_terminal__checking if at least one transmission_collision_condition is met and accordingly generating a checking_result__deriving_statistic_data from a plurality of checking_results_generated in response to a plurality of retry_packets_transmitted from the first wireless_communication_terminal  and detecting the transmission_collision between the first wireless_communication_terminal and the second wireless_communication_terminal according to the statistic_data.
