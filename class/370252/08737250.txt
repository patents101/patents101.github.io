Provided is an apparatus and method for tracking a position of a fixed_node. The position_tracking_apparatus of the fixed_node may include  an interface to extract  from a database in response to the occurrence of an event about tracking the position of the fixed_node__packet_data corresponding to a mobile_node associated with the event  and a processor to analyze a received_signal_strength of the packet_data and a position of the mobile_node from the packet_data  and to track the position of the fixed_node using the analysis_result.
