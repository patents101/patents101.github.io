A system and method for allowing a computing_device to associate_wirelessly with one or more access_points that are connected to a cable TV network when such device is in a range of such access_point  even though such wireless_device would not generally be authorized to receive_content or data from the access_point. Signals to and from the wireless_device are carried over the cable TV network and transmitted to other devices associated with other access_points_attached to the cable TV network.
