Methods  systems  and computer_readable_media for rapid_decoding of wireless_communications_network_uplink_data are disclosed. According to one method  the wireless_communications_network_data_analyzer_receives_downlink_data_generated by a wireless_access_node. The wireless_communications_network_data_analyzer_extracts_uplink_data_decode_keys from the downlink_data. The wireless_communications_network_data_analyzer_stores the uplink_data_decode_keys. The wireless_communications_data_analyzer_captures_uplink_data and decodes the uplink_data using the stored_uplink_data_decode_keys.
