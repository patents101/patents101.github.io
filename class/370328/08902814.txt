Provided is a multimedia_broadcast and multicast_service_controlling_apparatus and method_based on a user_location. The multimedia_broadcast and multicast_service_controlling_apparatus may receive MCCH information  may periodically_update a service_list of a mobile_terminal_based on the received MCCH information  the service_list_listing_services that are providable  may register a service in a pending_list when the service is not included in the updated_service_list and is requested to be provided  and may provide the service_registered in the pending_list when the mobile_terminal_reaches a location where the mobile_terminal may provide the service_registered in the pending_list.
