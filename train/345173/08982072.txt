There are provided a mobile_device and a method of controlling a screen of the mobile_device. The mobile_device includes  a display_unit_including an effective_display_area for displaying a screen  a touch_screen_panel_including a plurality of sensing_electrodes  and a control_unit_detecting a touch_input_based on a sensing_signal_generated from the plurality of sensing_electrodes and controlling the screen_displayed by the display_unit  wherein when the touch_input_corresponds to a previously_set first input_gesture  the control_unit_controls such that a size of the screen is reduced at a predetermined first ratio and the reduced_screen is only displayed in a portion of the effective_display_area.
