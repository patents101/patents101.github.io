Systems and methods for operating a handheld_computing_device having more than one input_device. An input_event may be received at one of the input_devices. The location of the input_event may be translated into a location with respect to a global_virtual_space. This translated_location may in turn be passed to an application_executing on the device. Additionally  systems and methods are provided for recognition of a gesture_input_spanning more than one independent_input_device.
