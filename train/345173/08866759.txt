The display_device with a touch_panel according to the present_invention has high_reliability and is a display_device with a touch_panel having a display_panel and a touch_panel placed on the front_surface of the display_panel  wherein the display_panel has a first substrate and a second substrate_provided so as to be layered on the first substrate  the first substrate has a non-overlapping_region that does not overlap the second substrate  and the touch_panel is a liquid_crystal_display_device with a touch_panel_secured on the second substrate  the touch_panel has an overlapping_region that overlaps the non-overlapping_region of the first substrate  and a spacer is secured to the touch_panel between the non-overlapping_region of the first substrate and the overlapping_region of the touch_panel.
