An input_device and computing_system is provided for a user to input_data. An input_device may comprise a touch_sensitive_pad and a hinge. The touchpad may have a touch_sensitive_surface. The touchpad may have a first end and a second end. The hinge may be disposed_parallel to the first end and the second end. The touchpad may be pivotably_connected to the hinge to allow displacement of the touchpad from a flat_position to an activate_position at the first end when a force is applied to the first end of the touch_surface.
