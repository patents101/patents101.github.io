The described embodiments allow for the selection of content in a touch_screen_display. The method includes detecting a single_tap on a word of text in a text_editing_field in a touch_screen  and responsive to the single_tap_selecting the word of text in whole. In another embodiment_additional_content can be selected. Contact with selected_content_displayed in a touch_screen can be detected. The contact with the touch_screen can be detected_moving in a direction along the selected_content. Additional content is selected by selecting a word of text_adjacent to the selected_content being contacted.
