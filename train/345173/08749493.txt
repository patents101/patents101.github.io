An input_device is disclosed. The input_device includes a movable_touch_pad_capable of detecting an object in close_proximity_thereto so as to generate a first control_signal. The input_device also includes a movement_indicator_capable of detecting the movements of the movable_touch_pad so as to generate one or more distinct second control_signals. The control_signals being used to perform_actions in an electronic_device_operatively_coupled to the input_device.
