The disclosed_subject_matter_relates to an architecture that can leverage Universal Mobile Telecommunication System  UMTS  Terrestrial Radio Access Network  UTRAN  features to facilitate more efficient or more robust_communication with a femtocell_network. In particular  features such as a UMTS localized common pilot channel along with various UTRAN features can enable_communications to be directed to specific_targets such as to a specific home nodeB  HNB  or to a specific_mobile_device_served by the HNB  while also reducing_macro_network_load.
