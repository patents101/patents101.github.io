A wireless_access_point_device_configured to serve_wireless_client_devices in a wireless_network_monitors_transmissions sent by direct_clients_capable of forming_ad_hoc_networks with one or more other direct_clients. Profile data is stored for each detected_direct_client. The profile_data indicates device_type and services of the direct_client  as well as potentially other information. The wireless_access_point_receives from a direct_client a request for information concerning one or more other direct_clients  and sends a response to the direct_client containing information for other direct_clients_based on the stored_profile_data.
