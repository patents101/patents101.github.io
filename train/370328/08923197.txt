A base_station_transmits a plurality of primary synchronization channel  PSC  symbols to a mobile_station in a slot of a subframe of a frame-based_wireless_communication_system. The base_station also transmits a plurality of secondary synchronization channel  SSC  symbols to the mobile_station in the slot of the subframe. The mobile_station_determines a preferred_receiver_beam_based on the plurality of received consecutive PSC symbols.
