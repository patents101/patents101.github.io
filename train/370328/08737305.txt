A method and apparatus for efficiently_allocating_resources to a terminal in a random_access_system are provided. A method for transmitting an abnormal_power down report from a Machine to Machine  M2M  device in a wireless_communication_system may include transmitting a Bandwidth Request  BR  preamble sequence and a first message to a base_station__receiving an uplink_resource_allocation from the base_station__transmitting the abnormal_power down report using the uplink_resource_allocation  and starting a first timer for confirmation of the transmitted_abnormal_power down report  wherein the first message may include an identifier of the M2M device and a BR index for transmitting an abnormal_power down report.
