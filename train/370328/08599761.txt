A mobile_station for use in a wireless_network. The mobile_station_transmits_feedback_values in a physical uplink control channel  PUCCH  to a base_station of the wireless_network. The mobile_station is operable to transmit to the base_station a subband second precoder matrix index  PMI  associated with a particular subband and a subband channel quality indicator  CQI  value associated with the particular subband together in a subframe of the PUCCH.
