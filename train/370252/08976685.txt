A priority_allocation_system may be adapted to regulate the priority which different users of a wireless_mobile_communication_system are given to one or more of its various services. It may include a priority_level_data_storage_system  a priority_enforcement_system  an administrator_interface  and a subscriber_interface. The priority_data_storage_system may be configured to store_priority_information for each of a plurality of subscriber_groups. Each subscriber_group may be assigned to one or more wireless_mobile_communication_devices. The priority_information for each subscriber_group may be indicative of one or more priorities_relating to use of the mobile_communication_system by all of the wireless_mobile_communication_devices which are assigned to the subscriber_group.
