An open_wireless_access_network_apparatus includes an Internet protocol  IP  resource management unit to allocate IPs  respectively  to a plurality of provider_servers and open wireless access points  APs  and map the IP of each provider_server to the IP of each open wireless AP  a subscriber_movement_management_unit to provide a second wireless AP with an IP that has been allocated to a first wireless AP when a mobile_device changes from one area in which the mobile_device has a signal_delivered from the first wireless AP to another area in which the mobile_device has a signal_delivered from the second wireless AP  and a wireless AP connection control unit to allow the mobile_device to be connected to the second wireless AP through the allocated IP.
