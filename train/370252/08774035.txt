Application requirements of an application_running on a wireless_device are received. The wireless_device is handed off from a first network to a second network when a first handoff_criteria is met_based on the application_requirements  first network_conditions  and second network_conditions. A second network_usage of the application is limited_based on the application_requirements and the second network_conditions  and first network_conditions are monitored while the application is running on the wireless_device in communication with the second network. The wireless_device is handed off from the second network to the first network when a second handoff_criteria is met_based on the application_requirements  the first network_conditions and the second network_conditions.
