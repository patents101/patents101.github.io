Systems and methods are provided for controlling_monitoring_operations  such as the monitoring of signal_strength__media_access_control_layer_overhead_information_data__application_layer_overhead_information  and/or data in a device  such as a battery_powered__mobile__communication_device. A user_interface_status and/or a flow_status can be monitored. Based at least in part on the user_interface_status and/or the flow_status  a determination can be made as to when and/or how often monitoring is to be performed.
